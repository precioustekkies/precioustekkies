## Citing Specific Articles from the Journal

When citing a specific article published in JBB, use this format:

### APA 7th Edition (Article Citation)

Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year). Title of article. *Journal of Biochemistry and Biophysics*, *Volume*(Issue), page range. https://doi.org/xx.xxxx/xxxxx

**Example:**
Smith, J. D., & Johnson, M. K. (2024). Protein folding mechanisms in cellular environments. *Journal of Biochemistry and Biophysics*, *7*(2), 45-62. https://doi.org/10.23953/jbb.1234567

### Vancouver Style (Article Citation)

Author AA, Author BB. Title of article. J Biochem Biophys. Year;Volume(Issue):page range. doi: xx.xxxx/xxxxx

**Example:**
Smith JD, Johnson MK. Protein folding mechanisms in cellular environments. J Biochem Biophys. 2024;7(2):45-62. doi: 10.23953/jbb.1234567

### MLA 9th Edition (Article Citation)

Author, First Name, and First Name Last Name. "Title of Article." *Journal of Biochemistry and Biophysics*, vol. Volume, no. Issue, Year, pp. page range, doi:xx.xxxx/xxxxx.

**Example:**
Smith, John D., and Mary K. Johnson. "Protein Folding Mechanisms in Cellular Environments." *Journal of Biochemistry and Biophysics*, vol. 7, no. 2, 2024, pp. 45-62, doi:10.23953/jbb.1234567.
